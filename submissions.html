Submissions

Submissions for the 2023-2024 issue are now open.

Our staff is currently accepting submissions of:

  • Poetry
  • Fiction
  • Creative Nonfiction
  • Screenwriting
  • Drama
  • Visual Art
  • Photography
  • Recorded performance (dance/choreography, theatre, spoken-word, performance art, poetry slam, recorded readings, etc.)
  • Audiovisual/multimedia submissions (music videos, digital art, student films, etc.)
  • Please Email submissions to onetwentyonejournal@gmail.com
  • as a single .doc or .docx attachment. The file name should contain your full name (JaneSmithpoetry.docx).
  • Please Indicate the genre of work in the email subject line (e.g. Poetry Submission), and include contact information and a brief
  • biographical statement in the body paragraph of your email.
  • Poems: send up to six. No page limit, within reason
  • Short fiction/Creative nonfiction: any number of pieces, totaling no more than twenty-five pages.
  • Drama/screenplays: Any number of pieces, totaling no more than fifty pages.
  • Recordings/Performances/Multimedia art: send up to three as digital files or web links, totaling no more than ten minutes.
  • Visual art/photography: send up to six as digital files.
  • We welcome previously published material as well as simultaneous submissions, as long as you promptly withdraw your work upon acceptance elsewhere.
  • All rights revert to the author upon original publication in One Twenty-One.
